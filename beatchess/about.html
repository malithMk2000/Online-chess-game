About Beatchess.com

Welcome to Beatchess.com, the ultimate platform where users can make real-money chess challenges and earn money by showcasing their chess skills! Whether you're a seasoned chess player or just looking to enjoy a game with a little competition, Beatchess.com is the place for you.

Players can challenge others by placing monetary bets and engage in exciting chess matches. With our secure platform, players can win real money based on their performance. The platform offers a seamless and enjoyable experience, ensuring that chess enthusiasts from around the world can connect and compete in a fun and fair environment.

Beatchess.com is developed and maintained by CODEZEN, a tech startup passionate about building innovative software solutions. We aim to create an engaging and competitive chess community while providing a user-friendly and secure platform for our users.
