Frequently Asked Questions

Q & A

1. How do I create an account on BeatChess?

You can register by clicking the 'Sign Up' button on the homepage and filling out the registration form with your details.

2. How do I challenge another player?

Once logged in, go to the challenge section, set the bet amount, and invite another player. Wait for them to accept the challenge to begin the game.

3. How is the winner determined?

The winner is determined based on the standard rules of chess. If the opponent resigns, runs out of time, or gets checkmated, you win.

4. What happens if there is a draw?

If the match ends in a draw, the bet amount will be refunded equally to both players.

5. How long does it take to process my winnings?

Winnings are usually credited instantly. However, in some cases, it may take a few minutes due to server traffic or processing delays.
